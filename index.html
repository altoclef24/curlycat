Cornish Rex

The Cornish Rex is known for its unique appearance and curly fur. They are extremely playful, and love to play with their human family members. They are also very affectionate, which makes them the perfect companion to have. They have quite a graceful appearance, with high cheekbones, large ears, and slender bodies. The first Cornish Rex was discovered Cornwall, England, by the owner, Nina Ennismore, when her British Shorthair gave birth to a male kitten with curly fur.
